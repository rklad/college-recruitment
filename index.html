Student Life/Campus

Talley Student Union at NC State serves as a vibrant hub for student life, offering a variety of dining options, meeting spaces, and recreational facilities. With its modern design and welcoming atmosphere, it fosters community engagement and provides a perfect spot for students to relax and connect.

NC State is home to over 700 clubs and extracurricular activities, catering to a wide range of interests, from academic and professional organizations to cultural and recreational groups. Students can also participate in various intramural and club sports, fostering teamwork and competition in a fun environment. With a strong emphasis on school spirit, NC State's varsity athletics provide an exciting way for students to support their teams and engage with the campus community.

NC State offers a variety of housing options, including traditional residence halls, suite-style living, and apartment-style accommodations to suit different preferences and lifestyles. With a focus on community and convenience, many of the residence halls are strategically located near dining facilities, academic buildings, and recreational spaces. Housing costs at NC State for the 2023-2024 academic year typically range from about $3,500 to $7,500 per year.

Cost & Financial Aid

In-State Tuition: About $9,100 per year


Out-of-State Tuition: Around $31,400 per year

NC State offers a wide range of financial aid opportunities, including scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs to help students finance their education. The university is committed to making higher education accessible, providing resources and support to guide students through the financial aid application process.
